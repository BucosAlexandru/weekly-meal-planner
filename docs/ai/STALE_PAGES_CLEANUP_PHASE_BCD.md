## Were Vercel Redirects Needed?

**No.** All 32 stale paths meet the redirect-not-needed criteria:

1. **Not in sitemap** — Google has no sitemap signal pointing to these URLs.
2. **Zero inbound links** from active plan pages (Phase A) or the recipe index pages.
3. **Not known to be externally linked** — these were slug-garbled internal URLs (e.g. `ciorb-de-burt`, `pl-cint-a-ciobanului`) with very low probability of external bookmarking or sharing.

If a user had directly bookmarked one of these URLs, they will receive a 404. Given the garbled nature of the slugs and the site's early stage, this risk is accepted.
