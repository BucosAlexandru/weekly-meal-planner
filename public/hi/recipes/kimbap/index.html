Kimbap

फ्रीज किया जा सकता है

मील प्रेप के लिए बढ़िया

सामग्री 4 सर्विंग्स

  • 2 cups short-grain white rice (uncooked, makes ~4 rolls)
  • 4 sheets toasted nori (20×18 cm)
  • 4 strips yellow pickled radish (danmuji), cut to nori length
  • 100g baby spinach
  • 2 medium carrots, julienned (about 150g)
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 200g imitation crab sticks (or cooked ham), split lengthwise
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • Bamboo rolling mat + plastic wrap

बनाने का तरीका

  1. 1Cook rice with 2¼ cups water: bring to boil, cover, simmer lowest heat 12 minutes, rest off heat 10 minutes.
  2. 2While hot, season with 1½ tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp salt, and sesame seeds; fold gently — do not stir.
  3. 3Blanch spinach 30 seconds in boiling salted water, squeeze very dry, season with ½ tsp sesame oil.
  4. 4Stir-fry julienned carrot 2 minutes in a dry pan with a pinch of salt.
  5. 5Beat 3 eggs with a pinch of salt, cook as a thin flat omelette in a lightly oiled pan, cut into 4 long strips.
  6. 6Lay a nori sheet shiny-side down on the bamboo mat.
  7. 7With wet fingers spread a thin, even layer of rice over ¾ of the nori, leaving a 4 cm gap at the far edge.
  8. 8Line fillings in a neat row along the near edge: spinach, carrot, crab stick, egg strip, and danmuji.
  9. 9Lift the near edge of the mat and roll firmly forward, pressing as you go; press the last 4 cm of bare nori to seal.
  10. 10Rub ½ tsp sesame oil on the outside.
  11. 11Slice with a sharp, lightly wet knife into 8 rounds in one clean push.
  12. 12Serve immediately or wrap in foil; best within 4 hours.
