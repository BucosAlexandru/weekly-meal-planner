जल्दी तैयार होता है

30 मिनट में तैयार

सामग्री 4 सर्विंग्स

  • 2 cups short-grain white rice
  • 200g beef sirloin, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 medium courgette, julienned
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 4 shiitake mushrooms, stems removed
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Sesame seeds to garnish

बनाने का तरीका

  1. 1Cook rice in 2.5 cups water: bring to a boil, cover and simmer on the lowest heat for 12 minutes, then rest off the heat for 10 minutes.
  2. 2Marinate beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp sugar and half the garlic for 15 minutes.
  3. 3Blanch spinach 30 seconds in boiling salted water, squeeze dry, season with 1 tsp sesame oil and salt; sauté bean sprouts 2 minutes with garlic and salt; stir-fry carrot and courgette separately for 2 minutes each with a drizzle of sesame oil.
  4. 4Sear marinated beef over high heat 3–4 minutes until caramelised; slice shiitake and sauté 2 minutes with a splash of soy sauce; fry eggs sunny-side up in sesame oil.
  5. 5Divide rice among 4 bowls, arrange vegetables and beef in sections, place an egg in the centre, add 1 tbsp gochujang per bowl, drizzle with sesame oil and scatter sesame seeds — mix everything together before eating..
