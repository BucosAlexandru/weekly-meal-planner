Tom Yum

मील प्रेप के लिए बढ़िया

सामग्री 6 सर्विंग्स

  • 1L chicken or seafood stock
  • 300g raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, cut into 3cm pieces and bruised
  • 30g galangal (or ginger), thinly sliced
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves
  • 3–4 Thai red chillies, halved
  • 200g oyster or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 tbsp tom yum paste (optional)
  • 1 spring onion stalk, to garnish
  • Fresh coriander to serve

बनाने का तरीका

  1. 1Bring stock to a medium simmer, add bruised lemongrass, galangal slices and kaffir lime leaves; cook 10 minutes to infuse the aromatics.
  2. 2Add tom yum paste (if using), chillies and mushrooms; cook 3–4 minutes until the mushrooms soften slightly.
  3. 3Add prawns and cook exactly 2–3 minutes until they turn pink and curl — do not overcook or they will become rubbery.
  4. 4Remove from heat and season with fish sauce, lime juice and palm sugar; taste and adjust the balance — the soup should be sour, salty, spicy and lightly sweet all at once.
  5. 5Serve immediately in deep bowls, garnished with spring onion and fresh coriander; the lemongrass, galangal and lime leaves are aromatics and not meant to be eaten.
