Japanese Curry Rice

मील प्रेप के लिए बढ़िया

सामग्री 4 सर्विंग्स

  • 2 cups short-grain rice (uncooked)
  • 400g boneless skinless chicken thighs (or beef chuck), cut into 3 cm pieces
  • 2 large onions, cut into medium dice
  • 2 medium potatoes, cut into 3 cm cubes
  • 2 medium carrots, cut into 1.5 cm rounds
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 800ml water
  • 1 pack Japanese curry roux blocks (e.g. S&B Golden Curry, medium, approx. 100g)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp ketchup (optional, for depth)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

बनाने का तरीका

  1. 1Cook rice with 2½ cups water: bring to boil, cover, lowest heat 13 minutes, rest off heat 10 minutes.
  2. 2In a large pot heat oil over medium heat.
  3. 3Add onions and cook, stirring often, for 15–20 minutes until soft and golden — do not skip this step; caramelised onions are the backbone of flavour.
  4. 4Add chicken, season with salt and pepper, sear until browned on all sides, 3–4 minutes.
  5. 5Add carrots and potatoes, stir 1 minute.
  6. 6Pour in 800ml water, bring to a boil, reduce to a gentle simmer uncovered for 15 minutes, or until potato is just tender.
  7. 7Remove pot from heat.
  8. 8Break roux blocks into small pieces and stir into the pot until fully dissolved — adding off heat prevents lumps.
  9. 9Return to low heat and simmer, stirring frequently, for 10 minutes until sauce is thick and glossy.
  10. 10Stir in Worcestershire sauce and ketchup, adjust seasoning.
  11. 11Serve over hot rice..
