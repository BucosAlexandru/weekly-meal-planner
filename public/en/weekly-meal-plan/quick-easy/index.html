How to use this meal plan

The Quick & Easy – max 30 min meal plan is designed for 2 people, with an estimated budget of €35–47 per week. You can adjust quantities for more people directly in the app.

Click "Open in app" to automatically load all meals into the planner. You can modify any recipe, add or remove days, and download the shopping list as a PDF — free (1 PDF/day) or unlimited with a subscription.

Why plan meals in advance?

  • Reduce food waste by up to 30% — buy only what you need
  • Save time and money — one shopping trip per week
  • Eat healthier — decisions made calmly, not when hungry
  • Eliminate the daily stress of "what should I cook today?"
