Latin America – Spicy & Vibrant

Mexico, Peru, Brazil and Cuba on your plate. Black beans, corn, avocado, spiced meats and vibrant colours.

2 people 7 days ~€42–55 / week 151 ingredients

Day Lunch Dinner
Monday Tamale

500g ground beef (80% lean), 8 small corn tortillas (15cm), 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 2 ripe tomatoes, finely diced (for pico de gallo)…
Tuesday Arroz Chaufa
500g long-grain rice, cooked and cooled (preferably day-old), 300g chicken breast or pork loin, cut into 1.5 cm cubes, 3 large eggs, beaten, 1 red bell pepper, finely diced, 6 spring onions, white parts finely sliced, green parts reserved… 		Feijoada
400g dried black beans, soaked overnight, 400g smoked pork ribs, 200g chourico or smoked sausage, sliced, 200g smoked bacon, diced, 1 large onion, chopped…
Wednesday Lomo Saltado
600g beef sirloin or tenderloin, cut into 1 cm strips, 3 large potatoes (about 700g), cut into thick fries, 1 large red onion, cut into 1 cm-thick wedges, 2 medium Roma tomatoes, cut into wedges (or thick slices), 1 yellow ají amarillo pepper (or 1 red bell pepper + 1 tsp ají amarillo paste)… 		Chili con carne
500g beef mince (min. 20% fat), 400g canned red kidney beans, drained, 400g canned chopped tomatoes, 2 large onions, chopped, 3 garlic cloves, minced…
Thursday Picadillo
ground beef, onion, garlic, tomatoes, pepper… 		Moqueca
800 g firm white fish fillets (sea bass, cod, or monkfish), cut into 5 cm chunks, 2 limes, juice only, 1 tsp salt, 1 large onion (~180 g), thinly sliced, 4 garlic cloves, finely minced…
Friday Pozole
hominy corn, pork, garlic, onion, chili pepper… 		Ropa Vieja
700 g beef flank steak or brisket, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 large onion (~200 g), thinly sliced, 1 red bell pepper and 1 green bell pepper (~150 g each), deseeded and sliced, 4 garlic cloves, finely minced…
Saturday Pupusa
400 g masa harina (nixtamalized corn flour, such as Maseca), plus extra, 350 ml warm water (about 40°C), plus more if needed, 1 tsp fine sea salt, 200 g canned refried black or pinto beans, warmed, 200 g queso quesillo or mozzarella, coarsely grated… 		Bandeja Paisa
rice, beans, beef, egg, plantain…
Sunday Arepa
For the dough:, 400g harina P.A.N. (pre-cooked white corn flour), 500ml warm water, 1 tsp salt, 2 tbsp vegetable oil… 		Chiles en nogada
4 large poblano chiles (~700 g total), 400 g ground pork, 2 tbsp olive oil or lard, 1 medium onion (~150 g), finely diced, 3 garlic cloves, minced…
