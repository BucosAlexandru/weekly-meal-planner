Saturday Kimbap
2 cups short-grain white rice (uncooked, makes ~4 rolls), 4 sheets toasted nori (20×18 cm), 4 strips yellow pickled radish (danmuji), cut to nori length, 100g baby spinach, 2 medium carrots, julienned (about 150g)… 		Rendang
1 kg beef chuck or brisket, cut into 5 cm cubes, 800 ml full-fat coconut milk (2 x 400 ml cans), 5 shallots (about 150 g), roughly chopped, 4 garlic cloves, roughly chopped, 2 tbsp fresh ginger (about 3 cm piece), roughly chopped…
Sunday Okonomiyaki

chicken breast, coconut milk, lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves…
