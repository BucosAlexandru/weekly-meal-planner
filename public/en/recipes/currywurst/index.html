Currywurst

Traditional recipe from Germany.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Ketchup
  • Curry powder
  • French fries (optional)

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Fry sausages.
  3. Slice them, and serve with a tomato sauce mixed with curry powder.
