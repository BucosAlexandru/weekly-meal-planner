Tamale

Traditional recipe from Mexico. Fresh ingredients and simple preparation.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Lard
  • Water
  • Meat or vegetable filling
  • Corn husks

👨‍🍳 How to make it

Mix cornmeal with lard and water, place filling on corn husks, roll up and steam until set.
