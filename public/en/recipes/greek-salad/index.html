Greek Salad

Traditional Greek salad — horiatiki — is the soul of summer tavernas: sun-warm tomatoes, crisp cucumber, a slab of feta, Kalamata olives and a generous pour of olive oil with wild oregano.

Total time35m
Active time25m
Servings6

Ingredients 6 servings

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, thickly sliced
  • 1 large cucumber, sliced
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red onion, thinly ringed
  • 200 g block feta
  • 100 g Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried Greek oregano
  • Coarse salt

How to make it

  1. 1Toss tomatoes, cucumber, pepper and onion in a bowl.
  2. 2Salt.
  3. 3Place the feta block on top whole (don't crumble — Greeks serve it as a slab).
  4. 4Scatter the olives.
  5. 5Drizzle generously with olive oil and shower with oregano.
  6. 6Serve immediately, no tossing.
