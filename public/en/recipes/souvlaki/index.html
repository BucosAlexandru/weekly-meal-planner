Souvlaki

Traditional recipe from Greece.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Marinate the pork pieces in olive oil, lemon, garlic, and oregano.
  3. Skewer and grill them until golden.
  4. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
