Chakhokhbili

Traditional recipe from Georgia. Fresh ingredients and simple preparation.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Coriander

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Fry onion and chicken, add bell pepper and tomatoes, cook slowly with spices and herbs.
  2. Serve warm, optionally with bread..
