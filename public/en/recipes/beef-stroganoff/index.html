Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff comes from the household of Count Stroganov in 19th-century St Petersburg — tender beef strips in a sauce of sour cream, mushrooms and mustard, fortifying for Russian winters. It became an international classic in the 20th century.

Ingredients 6 servings

  • 600 g beef tenderloin, sliced into strips
  • 300 g button mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp mustard
  • 200 ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper, parsley

How to make it

  1. 1Sauté onion and garlic in butter, add sliced mushrooms and brown.
  2. 2Add the beef strips, cook 3 minutes to colour.
  3. 3Pour in stock, reduce by half.
  4. 4Off the heat, stir in sour cream and mustard — no more boiling so the sauce doesn't split.
  5. 5Salt, pepper, parsley.
  6. 6Serve over mash or rice.
