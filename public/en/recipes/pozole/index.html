Pozole

Traditional recipe from Mexico.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Hominy corn
  • Pork
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Chili pepper
  • Cabbage
  • Radish
  • Lime

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Boil pork with hominy corn, onion and garlic.
  2. Add chili pepper, then serve with cabbage, radish, and lime..
