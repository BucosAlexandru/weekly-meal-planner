Pancakes

American pancakes are a traditional recipe from the USA.

Ingredients 4 servings

  • 200g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 240ml whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 30g melted butter, plus extra for the pan
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

How to make it

  1. 1In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  2. 2In a second bowl, whisk milk, eggs, melted butter and vanilla.
  3. 3Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and fold gently with a spatula — a few lumps are fine and even desirable; over-mixing develops gluten and makes the pancakes tough.
  4. 4Rest the batter 5 minutes: the baking powder bubbles will rise to the surface.
  5. 5Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, add a small knob of butter and wipe away the excess.
  6. 6Pour one ladle (about 80ml) of batter per pancake.
  7. 7Cook 2-3 minutes until bubbles break on the surface and the edges look set and dry.
  8. 8Flip and cook 1-2 minutes more until golden on the underside.
  9. 9Serve immediately — pancakes deflate as they cool.
  10. 10Top with real maple syrup, fresh butter and seasonal fruit..
💡
Tip: Let the dessert cool completely before serving for the best texture and flavour.
