Caprese Salad

Insalata Caprese takes its name from the island of Capri, where it was created in the 1920s as a tribute to the Italian flag — red, white and green — from sweet tomatoes, buffalo mozzarella and fresh basil.

Ingredients 4 servings

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 250 g buffalo mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Coarse salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)

How to make it

  1. 1Prepare all ingredients: wash, peel and cut everything as needed for the recipe.
  2. 2On a plate, alternate slices of tomato and mozzarella, tucking basil leaves between.
  3. 3Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  4. 4Serve at room temperature — straight from the fridge the mozzarella loses its creaminess.
