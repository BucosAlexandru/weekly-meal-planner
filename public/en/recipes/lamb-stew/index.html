Lamb Stew

Lamb stew is a universal cold-weather dish — meat slowly tenderised alongside root vegetables and herbs, until the whole pot becomes one warm aroma.

Active time20m
Servings6

Ingredients 6 servings

  • 800 g lamb shoulder, cubed
  • 2 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 500 ml beef stock
  • Thyme, bay leaf
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

How to make it

  1. 1Brown the lamb cubes in oil in a heavy pot, set aside.
  2. 2Sweat onions and garlic, stir in tomato paste.
  3. 3Return lamb, pour in stock, add thyme and bay.
  4. 4Simmer covered on low for 90 minutes.
  5. 5Add carrots and potatoes, cook 30 more minutes until the meat is fork-tender.
