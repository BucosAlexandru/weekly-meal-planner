Spanakopita

Traditional recipe from Greece.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Phyllo pastry
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 How to make it

Fill phyllo pastry with spinach and cheese mixture, roll or layer in a tray, brush with egg, and bake until golden.
