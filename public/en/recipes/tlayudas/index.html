Tlayudas

Traditional recipe from Mexico.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Large tortilla
  • Refried beans
  • Oaxaca cheese
  • Meat (usually beef, chicken, or chorizo)
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Salsa

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Spread beans on tortilla, add meat, cheese and vegetables.
  2. Bake or grill until crispy..
