Jerk Chicken

Traditional recipe from Jamaica.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Green onion
  • Chili pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Jerk spices
  • Lime

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Marinate chicken pieces with jerk spice mix.
  3. Grill or roast until crispy and flavorful.
  4. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
