Tagine

Traditional recipe from Morocco.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • Chickpeas
  • Dried apricots
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Cinnamon
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Slow-cook meat (usually lamb or chicken) with vegetables, dried fruits, spices, and a bit of water in a covered tagine pot.
  3. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
