Beshbarmak

Traditional recipe from Kyrgyzstan.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • Noodles
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Broth

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Boil meat (usually lamb or beef) until tender.
  3. Serve over homemade wide noodles with sautéed onion.
