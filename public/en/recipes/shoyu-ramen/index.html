Shoyu Ramen

Traditional recipe from Japan.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Ramen noodles
  • Soy sauce (shoyu)
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Chashu pork belly
  • Marinated egg
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Nori
  • Green onion
  • Narutomaki fish cake
  • Sesame oil

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Make chicken broth: simmer a chicken carcass with ginger, garlic, and onion for 3-4 hours, strain and keep the clear broth.
  2. Make shoyu tare: combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, and a little sugar; simmer gently for 10 minutes. This tare gives the soup its signature flavor.
  3. Roll pork belly and braise in soy sauce, mirin, and sugar for 1.5 hours to make chashu. Cool and slice thinly.
  4. Marinate soft-boiled eggs in soy sauce, mirin, and water for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).
  5. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions; drain well.
  6. Mix hot broth with shoyu tare to your taste — the balance is key.
  7. Place noodles in a bowl, pour broth over them, and garnish with chashu, marinated egg, bamboo shoots, nori, narutomaki, and green onion.
