Naengmyeon

Traditional recipe from North Korea.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Buckwheat noodles
  • Beef
  • Cucumber
  • Boiled egg
  • Kimchi
  • Ice
  • Vinegar

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Boil buckwheat noodles, rinse with cold water.
  3. Serve in cold broth with cucumber slices, boiled egg, and beef.
