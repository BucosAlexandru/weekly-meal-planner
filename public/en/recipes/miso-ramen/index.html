Miso Ramen

Traditional recipe from Japan.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Chicken or pork broth
  • Miso paste
  • Ramen noodles
  • Ground pork
  • Corn
  • Butter
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Marinated egg
  • Nori
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bean sprouts

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Sauté minced garlic in a little oil for 1 minute.
  2. Add ground pork and cook over medium-high heat until browned.
  3. Add miso paste and stir for 2 minutes to bloom the flavors.
  4. Pour in hot broth (chicken or pork) and stir well. Do not boil vigorously after adding miso.
  5. Add a knob of butter and a splash of soy sauce for richness.
  6. Cook ramen noodles separately according to instructions; drain well.
  7. Blanch bean sprouts in boiling water for 1 minute; drain.
  8. Fill bowls with noodles, ladle miso broth over them, and garnish with corn, bean sprouts, bamboo shoots, marinated egg, nori, sesame seeds, and green onion.
