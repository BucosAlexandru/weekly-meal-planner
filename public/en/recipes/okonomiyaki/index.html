Okonomiyaki

Traditional recipe from Japan.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cabbage
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Meat or seafood
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Spring onion

👨‍🍳 How to make it

Mix flour, eggs, cabbage, and optional ingredients, fry as a thick pancake, then serve with okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise.
