Pljeskavica

Traditional recipe from Serbia.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Mix ingredients, form large patties.
  3. Fry or grill and serve with salad or bun.
