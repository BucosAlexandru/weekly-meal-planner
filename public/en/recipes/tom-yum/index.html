Tom Yum

Ingredients 4 servings

  • Shrimp
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Lime leaves
  • Chili
  • Mushrooms
  • Fish sauce

How to make it

  1. 1Prepare all ingredients: wash, peel and cut everything as needed for the recipe..
  2. 2Boil broth with shrimp, mushrooms, lemongrass, lime, chili, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. 3Season with fish sauce and lime juice..
  4. 4Serve warm, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings..
💡
Tip: Fish is ready when the flesh turns opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
