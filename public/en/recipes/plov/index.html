Plov

Traditional recipe from Uzbekistan.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lamb or beef
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Fry meat and vegetables.
  3. Cook everything with rice and spices until water is absorbed.
  4. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
