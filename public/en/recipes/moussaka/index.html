Moussaka

Traditional recipe from Greece.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Potatoes
  • Minced meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bechamel sauce

👨‍🍳 How to make it
