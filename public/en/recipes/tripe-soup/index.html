Tripe Soup

Traditional recipe from Romania.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Beef tripe
  • Sour cream
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Boil the beef tripe with vegetables.
  3. Add garlic and vinegar for flavor.
  4. Finish with sour cream and eggs for a creamy soup.
  5. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
