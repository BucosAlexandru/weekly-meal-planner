Chakchouka

Traditional recipe from Tunisia.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Sauté onion, peppers and garlic.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Crack eggs on top and cook until set.
  5. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
