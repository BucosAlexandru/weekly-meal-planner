Sea Bass Provençal

Loup de mer à la provençale weds the Mediterranean sea bass with the vocabulary of Provence — cherry tomatoes, black olives, capers, garlic, herbs from the garrigue. One pan, one oven, one scent: summer on the coast.

Ingredients 6 servings

  • 2 sea bass fillets (about 200 g each)
  • 400 g cherry tomatoes
  • 12 black Niçoise olives
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • Fresh thyme
  • Olive oil, dry white wine
  • Salt, pepper, basil

How to make it

  1. 1Heat the oven to 200°C.
  2. 2In an oiled dish lay halved cherry tomatoes, olives, capers, sliced garlic, thyme and rosemary.
  3. 3Drizzle with olive oil and white wine.
  4. 4Place the fish fillets on top, season with salt and pepper.
  5. 5Bake 15-18 minutes until the fish is opaque and juices run from the tomatoes.
  6. 6Scatter fresh basil, serve with bread to mop the Provençal sauce.
