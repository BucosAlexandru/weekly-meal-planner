Fesenjan

Traditional recipe from Iran.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Walnuts
  • Pomegranate molasses
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Oil

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Brown pieces of meat (usually chicken or duck).
  3. Slowly cook them in a sauce of ground walnuts and pomegranate molasses until tender.
  4. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
