Lasagna

Lasagna is Italian pasta at its most generous — layers of sheets with meat sauce, béchamel and cheese, baked until the top is golden. The modern version draws from the Bolognese classic but has become an iconic dish worldwide.

Ingredients 6 servings

  • 300 g lasagna sheets
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g tomato sauce
  • 300 ml béchamel sauce
  • 150 g Parmigiano grated
  • 150 g mozzarella
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

How to make it

  1. 1Sauté onion and garlic, add the mince and brown well.
  2. 2Pour in tomato sauce, season and simmer 30 minutes.
  3. 3In a baking dish, layer: meat sauce, lasagna sheets, béchamel, mozzarella, Parmigiano.
  4. 4Repeat, finishing with a thick layer of béchamel and Parmigiano.
  5. 5Bake at 180°C for 35 minutes until golden.
