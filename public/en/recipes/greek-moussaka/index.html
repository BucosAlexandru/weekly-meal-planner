Greek Moussaka

Traditional recipe from Greece.

Add to my meal plan

🛒 Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Potatoes
  • Minced meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Bechamel sauce

👨‍🍳 How to make it

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients: wash and chop vegetables, cut meat if needed, measure spices.
  2. Layer eggplants, potatoes, and cooked minced meat with tomatoes and onion in a tray, cover with bechamel sauce, and bake until golden.
  3. Serve immediately while hot, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
