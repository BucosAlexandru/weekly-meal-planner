How to make it

  1. 1Prepare all ingredients: wash, peel and cut everything as needed for the recipe.
  2. 2Fry cooked rice with garlic, onion, soy sauce, vegetables, and meat or shrimp.
  3. 3Top with a fried egg.
  4. 4Serve warm, garnished with fresh herbs or your favourite toppings.
💡
Tip: Let the meat rest for 5 minutes after cooking before slicing or serving.
